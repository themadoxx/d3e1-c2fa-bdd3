Applications SaaS

Great copywriting tells a story, gives a feeling or forces action from your customers.

You don’t sell a ‘grey’ car. You sell car in ‘limited edition, gun-metal grey’.

The difference between the two descriptions above is ‘desire’. You created that purely with words.

The first car nobody wants, the second car everyone wants.

Physically it’s the same product – but what you say about it matters more. It moves the needle in terms of sales & revenue.

Good copywriting defies the laws of mathematics. Buy one get one free & 50% OFF is mathematically the same unit price… but one of those offers produces far more sales.

Copy can affect click through rate, open rate, conversion rate, and even affect how happy someone is when they get your product.

We once sold a product called Fiddle Stick and it bombed. We changed nothing else but the copywriting and then sold 21,000 units worth in one year.

To figure out if your copy is working for you – book a meeting here.

You never get a second chance at a first impression.

For this reason, your website is your strongest asset. It’s the thing that indicates credibility in your business.

Go tell a stranger on the street that you own a clothing brand. The first thing they’ll say is “What’s your website?”

They don’t assume you sell clothes from the back of a van or from a brick & mortar store. Their brain goes straight to ‘show me the website’.

So when they get there, it cannot let you down. You need it to be a good site.

It needs to look consistent with your brand. It needs to have the correct navigation, speedy loading times and be CRO optimized.

A terrible website suggests to customers that you’re a terrible brand… Which we know isn’t true.

But you can’t educate customers who leave. So you need to do it right, first time, every time.
