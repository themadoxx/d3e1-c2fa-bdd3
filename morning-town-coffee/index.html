Morning Town Coffee

Melbourne Coffee Roastery

Melbourne is world famous for it’s coffee culture, so when we developed a branding package and product line design for the Aussie roastery, with so much local competition, we knew we had to think outside the box.

Brewed to perfection

Part of the initial scope was to create a brand that both fit into the vibrant coffee scene, yet stood apart from it. After our in depth marketing research and assessing Morning Town’s offering, we selected our direction.

A marraige of clean, youth focus lettering and roughly textured minalistic illustrations created a strong aesthetic voice for the brand, speaking to active, millenial and gen Z buyer base by expressing individuality, quality, vibrancy and authenticity.

Morning town loved it and so do we.
